Micropolis: Views from the Miniature City


2023 Views from the Miniature City a performance in July at La MaMa Theater, which reimagines scenes from Skipitares’s seminal 1981 show "Micropolis: 6 Portraits and a Landscape,” in which a host of figures appear within various interiors characteristic of downtown New York City. The scenes were exhibited in June at 15 Orient Gallery in Bushwick. Reviewing the installation, the New York Times calls the show "a marvel which argues for the power of puppetry" as a contemporary art medium.
